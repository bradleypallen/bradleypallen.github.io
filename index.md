| [![Brad's LinkedIn page](resources/images/bpa_headshot.png)](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bpallen) | Brad Allen is [**a technology executive and serial entrepreneur**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bpallen) with over thirty-five years of experience bringing innovative software solutions to market. Brad is currently Chief Architect at Merit, a San Francisco Bay Area startup delivering verified identity services and solutions. Prior to Merit, Brad was Chief Architect at Elsevier, and was founder and CTO at three enterprise software startups in the Los Angeles area, achieving successful exits in two of the three.  |
